FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we get asked most often. Still unsure? Call or WhatsApp us directly.

How quickly can NOBSED Engineering start my project?

For most projects we can mobilise within 1–2 weeks of signing a contract and receiving a deposit. Emergency plumbing works can often be attended to within 24–48 hours depending on our current schedule. Contact us today to check availability.

Do you provide a written quotation before starting?

Absolutely. Every project begins with a free site assessment and a detailed, itemised written quotation. No work begins without your written approval of the quote. There are no hidden costs — what we quote is what you pay.

What areas do you serve in Ghana?

We are based in Agbogba, Accra and primarily serve Greater Accra including East Legon, Tema, Kasoa, Adenta, Madina, Spintex, Airport Hills and surrounding areas. For large-scale projects we can mobilise nationwide. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?

Yes. We work across all project types — from individual home plumbing repairs to multi-storey commercial buildings, housing estates, institutional facilities (churches, schools, hospitals) and government infrastructure. Our portfolio spans all scales.

What payment terms do you offer?

Payment is structured in agreed milestone instalments — typically an initial deposit, a mid-project payment, and a final balance on satisfactory completion. We accept mobile money (MTN, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money) and bank transfers. Full payment terms are set out clearly in our contract.

Is NOBSED Engineering registered and certified?

Yes. NOBSED Engineering Limited is a duly registered Ghanaian company. Our engineers hold professional licences and our operations comply with all applicable Ghana Standards Authority and construction industry regulations. We carry site insurance on all projects.
