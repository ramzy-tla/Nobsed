Who We Are

Proudly Ghanaian — Built on Trust

NOBSED Engineering Limited is a Ghanaian-owned and operated construction and engineering company, established in 2009 and headquartered in Agbogba, Accra. Over the past 16+ years, we have grown from a local plumbing outfit into one of Accra's most respected multi-disciplinary engineering firms.

We are dealers and experts in Plumbing, Civil Engineering, Building Construction, Mechanical Engineering, Tiling, and Sales of Blocks & Cement. Our reputation is built on consistent delivery — projects completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

From single residential homes to large-scale commercial developments, church construction, industrial facilities and government infrastructure, NOBSED Engineering brings the same commitment to every project: quality that lasts, service that matters.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2009

Company Founded

NOBSED Engineering Limited established in Agbogba, Accra — starting with residential plumbing and drainage services.

2011–2013

First Major Projects

Expanded into building construction and civil works. Delivered first residential estate projects in Greater Accra.

2015

Corporate Client Partnerships

Secured partnerships with Trasacco Properties and other major developers. Grew team to include licensed civil engineers.

2017–2019

Institutional Works

Completed construction and plumbing works for ICGC Jesus Temple and major commercial buildings across Accra.

2021–2025

Growth & Expansion

100+ completed projects. Expanded service divisions to include mechanical engineering, tiling, and cement supply. Now one of Accra's most trusted engineering firms.
